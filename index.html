Release

Luiz Henrique Costa began his career in music early. With musician parents, he was always in touch with music and at the age of 16 he discovered his passion for e-music. He started playing and producing in mid-2009, when he was listening to melodic Progressive House like Deadmau5, AdamK, Soha, among others. Until 2014 he worked with his other project "Rick Costa", which left the scene to make room for "Henrell", changing its musical identity and also the project's proposal.

In 2015 he participated in a Spinnin' Records Remix Contest, where he remixed “Chemicals” by Don Diablo and Tiesto. He won the second position in a ranking with more than a thousand participants, thus gaining recognition from Don who months later played his song "Truth" and others remixes and bootlegs on his Radio Show "Hexagon".

In the same year, Glorie Records appeared, a label created with friends Vee Brondi and Fred Blanck. In search of new talents for the market, they seek to bring news and quality music. The label has been growing in recent months, with the support of several international producers such as Thomas Gold, Ummet Ozcan, Max Vangeli, Anderblast, Blasterjaxx, Nicky Romero, Don Diablo, among others.
